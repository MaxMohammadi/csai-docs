# Database Development

The Database Development team maintains the connection between our APIs and the database. They ensure that incoming queries and mutations are handled correctly in a performant and secure fashion. They are also the gatekeepers of the SQL schema, defining how data is stored and retrieved.

## Contributions

- Architecting and maintain the database
- Building a clear contract between the APIs and the database
- Educating other teams on how to effectively fetch information

## Stack

- SQLAlchemy
- Flask
